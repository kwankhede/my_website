Nalanda Academy, Wardha

Nalanda Academy is an educational resource center for marginalized, rural, and non-English medium students. Since 2013, it has trained over 17,000 students from 15 states, enabling admissions to top universities in India and abroad. The academy offers all programs free of cost and is supported by a broad network of mentors, donors, and community volunteers[web:1][web:5].

  • New 9-acre residential campus under construction at Waifad, Wardha (Foundation Ceremony: Oct 5, 2025)[web:3]
  • Digital Nalanda platform delivering online education to 17,000+ students[web:1][web:6]
  • Abhiyan Labs & Libraries for holistic STEM and reading outreach [web:2][web:6]
  • Peer mentoring and career guidance from over 200 mentors[web:1][web:2]
