Nalanda Academy, Wardha

Founded in 2013, Nalanda Academy offers free education, mentorship, and resources to over 17,000 students from rural and marginalized backgrounds. The academy supports students' admissions to top universities and runs extensive programs like Digital Nalanda, Abhiyan Labs, and 15 rural libraries [web:1][web:5].

  • New 9-acre residential campus in Waifad, Wardha (Foundation Laying Ceremony: October 5, 2025) [web:3]
  • Over 200 mentors and 21 educators [web:1]
  • Digital Nalanda platform for online learning, reaching 17,000+ students [web:1][web:6]
  • Community outreach: Abhiyan Libraries, Labs, workshops, and STEM programs [web:2][web:6]
  • Admissions facilitated to premier Indian and foreign universities [web:2][web:5]
