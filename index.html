Wardha, MH, India



I am a business and social entrepreneur with 10+ years of experience in strategic planning, public relations, organizational management, and educational innovation.

I currently serve as the Associate Director and Co-founder of Nalanda Academy, Wardha — a pioneering institution that provides high-quality education to students from marginalized and underrepresented communities across India. In addition to leading strategic initiatives, I am heading our ongoing campus construction project, creating an inclusive and sustainable learning environment.

I teach mathematics and mentor students preparing for higher education and competitive exams, blending analytical rigor with a deep commitment to social justice. I am also deeply involved in Nalanda Abhiyan Labs (NAL), where we experiment with data-driven tools, build open-source education platforms, and research scalable models of grassroots transformation.

My academic background includes degrees in data science, finance, and engineering. I am an AI and machine learning enthusiast, constantly exploring how emerging technologies can drive equitable outcomes in education and society. Before Nalanda, I worked in the finance industry, focusing on risk modeling and data-based decision-making.

You can often find me at the gym, committed to fitness and well-being. I’m always looking to connect with fellow innovators, educators, and changemakers who care about community-driven impact and inclusive development.
