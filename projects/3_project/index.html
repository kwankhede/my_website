Nalanda Academy

A community-driven initiative empowering marginalized students through quality education since 2013

Nalanda Academy is a community-driven initiative dedicated to providing high-quality academic support to marginalized students across India. Starting with 1 educator and 22 students in 2013, we've grown to support over 18,000 students with 21 educators and 200 mentors.

Nalanda Campus Construction

Nalanda Academy is excited to announce the ongoing construction of our very own state-of-the-art campus in Wardha, Maharashtra. This dedicated facility aims to provide a vibrant learning environment with modern classrooms, advanced technology labs, community spaces, and extensive library resources to further empower our students.

As one of the leaders driving this project forward, I am deeply involved in the planning, coordination, and execution phases to ensure this campus meets the highest standards of educational excellence and community engagement. This new campus will significantly enhance our capacity to support thousands of students and mentors.

  • Located strategically in Wardha to serve regional and national students.
  • Sustainable and eco-friendly construction practices being implemented.
  • Facilities include classrooms, libraries, computer labs, and community halls.
  • Designed to accommodate growing numbers of students and educators.

Stay tuned for updates as this exciting chapter unfolds — building a future-ready Nalanda Academy campus to nurture generations of learners.
