Nalanda Academy Overview

Nalanda Academy is a community-driven initiative dedicated to providing high-quality academic support to marginalized students across India. With a strong emphasis on inclusion, the academy empowers students from underprivileged backgrounds to achieve their educational goals. Starting in 2013, with only one educator and 22 students, Nalanda Academy has grown to employ over 21 educators and 200 mentors, offering free education to over 18,000 students from 15 different states in India. The organization aims to bridge the gap in educational access and provide resources that enable students to pursue higher education both within India and internationally.
